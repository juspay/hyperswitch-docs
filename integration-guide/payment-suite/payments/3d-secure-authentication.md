# 3D Secure Authentication

### Overview

3D Secure (3DS) is an authentication protocol supported by all major card networks (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover). It adds a verification step between card authorization and capture — typically an OTP, biometric, or bank app approval — confirming that the person initiating the payment is the legitimate cardholder.
