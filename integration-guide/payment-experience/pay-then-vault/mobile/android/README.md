## Modify and Experiment

While the Unified Checkout is pre-optimized for maximum conversions, the Hyperswitch Android SDK empowers you to go beyond the standard. Take control with our SDK APIs, allowing you:

<table data-view="cards"><thead><tr><th></th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td><strong>Include New Fields</strong></td><td>Adapt swiftly to various use cases by adding new fields to the payment form, such as collecting billing addresses or zip codes for additional information.</td></tr><tr><td><strong>Prioritize Payment Methods</strong></td><td>Impact your payment mix and conversion rates by prioritizing or promoting specific payment methods based on your customer preferences.</td></tr><tr><td><strong>Switch Themes and Layouts</strong></td><td>Choose from a wide range of pre-designed themes and layouts for the checkout page, aligning the Unified Checkout with your app's aesthetics.</td></tr></tbody></table>
