# Payment Suite

Hyperswitch provides an open-source, lightweight and full-stack solution to effortlessly and effectively manage payments. It provides you with:

* **Payment Orchestration** – Unified API and no-code integrations for all 60+ payment processors and numerous payment methods. Enables improving authorisation rates with built-in intelligent routing.
* **Checkout Experience** – Seamless, fast, and intuitive checkout experience for end users, improving conversions.
* **Payment Operations** – Simplifies management of all payment workflows and operations from a centralised dashboard.
