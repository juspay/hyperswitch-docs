# Demystifying Pix Open Finance

_A practical guide for builders, designers, and compliance teams._

### Who this is for

<table data-card-size="large" data-view="cards"><thead><tr><th></th><th></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td><strong>Product &#x26; Strategy</strong></td><td>you are mapping scope, risks, and go-live requirements.</td></tr><tr><td><strong>Developers</strong></td><td>you are implementing an RP/Client that calls bank (ASPSP) APIs.</td></tr><tr><td><strong>Design &#x26; UX</strong></td><td>you are shaping consent and payment journeys for your app/website</td></tr><tr><td><strong>Compliance &#x26; Security</strong> </td><td>you are validating roles, certificates, and evidence.</td></tr></tbody></table>

### What you will achieve

By following the learning path below, you are:

1. understanding Brazil’s Open Finance pillars and Pix rails,
2. passing **OpenID/FAPI-BR RP** conformance,
3. onboarding to the **Directory** (getting software\_id & SSAs), and
4. initiating **Pix via Open Finance** (PISP redirection, Biometric/JSR, or Automático), end-to-end.
